About Me

I am an independent hobbyist photographer currently studying cinema at the University of Oregon to become a future cinematographer/director of photography, graduating with a bachelors degree by 2027. I am self taught in the ways of framing, color, editing, etc.

Brief History

I was born and raised on the coast of Oregon in a small town called Nehalem. I attended the nearby Neah-Kah-Nie High School and graduated in 2023. I am a first generation college student, along with my siblings.
